Big Data = Big Analysis

For my thesis at DSI, I am working with the Indian organization Spring Health. Their main aim goal and purpose is to provide clean drinking water to rural communities without access to clean freshwater.

Also in this repository is a csv file. I have conducted research with the customers of Spring Health, the ones who need and buy this service. I have also conducted a research process called 'self documentation', where 12 families participated in photographing their daily consumption of this water. I have a lot of data and now I would like to visualize this.

I want to make an interactive like Snowfall. This will include storytelling through pictures and images along with interactive graphs like this one. This will help me tell individual motivations for consumers along with their community beliefs.

I will create this using basic html and some java script. If I can figure out how to write the 'if' and 'else' statements, then I will be able to visualize what i have in mind.
